3. Creating the story

Job insecurity on the rise amongst young workers in the UK

The proportion of workers in insecure jobs between the age of 16 and 24 has risen from 11.4% in 2004 to 15.7% in 2019. Even prior to the coronavirus pandemic this trend has been impacting young people across the entire country. Other age categories have seen less of a shift towards increased job insecurity.
